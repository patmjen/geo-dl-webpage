Visual Computing Journal Club for Geometric Deep Learning

May 18, 2021
1 min read

Our aim with this journal club is to strengthen our knowledge of the cutting edge research in geometric deep learning. The current format is to meet every Tuesday from 14:30 to 15:30 where one of the participants presents a paper related to geometric deep learning which followed by discussion. The summary of the presentations will be added here as a post.
